Commentary

Now let's get into the different kinds of voice work that taken part in over the past few years. First is commentary, which I divide into two subcategories: Live Commentary and Pre-Recorded Commentary.

Live commentary is voiced commentary one provides on something that is taking place in real time. If you're a fan of sports, think the sports commentators who are talking about what is occuring during a match. For me, this was done on a streaming platform for gamers known as Twitch.

One of my favorite game franchises of all time is the Age of Empires series, particularly 2. I first played it when I was 5 years old and have played it on and off for the past 17 years. I recently reconnected with it and got to know some members of the community, some of whom actually stream over Twitch, either to play games live, give commentary over matches, or for some, both. I have connected with people from all over the world, such as Argentina, Germany, Scotland, Hungary, etc. This truly is a global community and I am happy to be a part of it.
